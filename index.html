About me

My name is Syed Gillani and currently I'm presuing my Post-Doc. I work at Laborotire Hubert Curien in Saint-Etienne, France and I'm part of the Connected Intelligence team. Prior to this, I completed my PhD from University Jean Monnet and my Master degree from the University of Edingburgh and University of Trento.

Research Interests

My research interests span across multiple domains including: core database technologies, graph databases, stream processing, knowledge-based systems, complex event processing and parallel and distributed systems.

Furthermore, I love programming – preferably in Java and C++ – and spend much of my free time juggling with new stuff -- which is a lot more fun than it sounds!
